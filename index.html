Welcome to MathClub Activities at KPRIT Annual Day Technical Fest

On behalf of the Math Club, we are organizing three exciting activities as part of our annual technical fest:

  • Treasure Hunt
  • Snake and Ladder
  • Math Mystery

All activities will challenge your logical thinking and problem-solving abilities through a series of aptitude-based questions covering the following topics:

  • Arithmetic
  • Decoding and Coding (Letter Series)
  • Calendar Problems
  • Problems on Ages
  • Blood Relations
  • Simple Interest
  • Probability
  • Number Series
  • Profit and Loss
  • Percentage

Treasure Hunt - Activity Details

This activity is designed for groups of two members. Once registered, the team will be contacted with further details.

Instructions:

Participants will be informed of the exact location 2 days before the event. At the venue, teams will solve 15 aptitude questions covering topics such as:

  • Logical Reasoning
  • Age-related problems
  • Calendar-based questions
  • Other aptitude challenges

Evaluation and Winners:

All teams will be evaluated based on their score from the aptitude test. The team with the highest score will be declared the winner and moved to the final location of the Treasure Hunt.
