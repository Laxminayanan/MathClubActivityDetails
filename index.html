Welcome to Number Ninjas Activities at KPRIT Annual Tech Fest 2k25

Exciting Math Activities Await You!

On Behalf Of Humanities & Science Department (Mathematics) , we are organizing three exciting activities as part of our annual technical fest:

  1. Logic Puzzle Race
  2. Math Mystery
  3. Math Escape Room

All activities will challenge your logical thinking and problem-solving abilities through a series of aptitude-based questions.

Activity 1: Logic Puzzle Race

Objective:

Put your critical thinking and problem-solving skills to the ultimate test in a thrilling race packed with logic challenges and mathematical twists!

Team Formation:

Each team must consist of 5 members.

Note⚠️: "Three members from the team will be answer the questions, one will roll the dice, and one will move Accordingly on the Logic Puzzle Race mat."

How to Play:

  • Roll the dice and move forward according to the number you get.
  • If you land on a square marked with a "?", prepare to answer a logic or math puzzle!
  • Answer correctly to stay on your square.
  • If Answered incorrectly? Move backward To the Same number of steps you Rolled.
  • You have 3 total chances — each wrong or skipped answer counts as one strike.
  • Three strikes and you're out!

Rules:

  • No calculators, mobile phones, or external help allowed.
  • Use only your brain — rely on logic and math skills!
  • Be respectful and fair to all players.

Winning Condition:

The First Team To Reach The Final Square is Declared As Winner!

Activity 2: Math Mystery

Objective:

Math Mystery is a level-based team challenge that tests your logic, teamwork, and curiosity through exciting math puzzles.

Team Formation:

    Each team must consist of 4 members.

Activity Flow:

  • Level Progression: The game has 4 levels, each increasing in difficulty.
  • Only one team member plays at the start, chosen by the team.

Level 1

  • One member (Participant A) plays.
  • In This Level The Participant Should Answer Single Question Without Taking The Help Of Other Team Members.
  • If Answered correctly: proceed to Level 2 By picking The second team member.
  • If Answered incorrectly: Team is eliminated.

Level 2

  • Two members collaborate.
  • In This Level Both (Participant A) and (Participant B) Can Work Together And Must Answer The Two Questions.
  • If Answered correctly: proceed to Level 3 By picking The Third team member.
  • If Answered incorrectly: Team is eliminated.

Level 3

  • The Three members Will be Going To solve the challenge.
  • In This Level (Participant A), (Participant B) and (Participant C) Can Work Together And Must Answer The Three Questions.
  • If Answered correctly: proceed to Level 4, with all four playing.
  • If Answered incorrectly: Team is eliminated.

Level 4 (Final)

  • In This Level All four members Will be Going To solve The Question.
  • Note: In This 4th Level (Last Level) There Will Be Question/Challenge Based On Sudoku.
  • In This Level (Participant A), (Participant B), (Participant C),(Participant D) Can Work Together And Must Answer The Given Question.
  • If Answered correctly: Team wins!

Winning Condition:

The team that completes all four levels correctly is declared the winner of Math Mystery!

Activity 3: Math Escape Room

Objective:

Dive into an exciting adventure filled with logic, clues, and math! This activity challenges your critical thinking, teamwork, and mathematical reasoning through a series of interactive, brain-teasing puzzles.

Team Formation:

Each team should consist of 3 to 5 members. A maximum of 2 members from each team are allowed to answer the questions displayed in the computer program.

Example 1: In a team of 3, all 3 members can participate in the treasure hunt, but only 2 members can answer the questions in the computer program.

Example 2: In a team of 5, all 5 members can join the treasure hunt, but only 2 members are permitted to answer the questions in the computer program.

The same rule applies to teams with 4 members.

How to Play:

  • Gadget Submission: Participants must submit their mobile phones and other electronic devices to the coordinators upon arrival.
  • The First Clue: Each team will receive a riddle or clue that leads to a hidden box or ball located somewhere on the campus.
  • Note⚠️: Participants must return to the Venue with the Hidden Ball Inside The Hidden box within 15 minutes.
  • The Secret Number: Inside the object, you'll find a number. Return to the venue with it.
  • System Setup: You'll be assigned a laptop or computer where the game begins.
  • Follow these steps:
    • Enter participants' names, email IDs, phone numbers, and any other details requested by the Computer program.
    • Read the rules carefully.
    • Press "YES" to start.
    • Enter the secret number you found.
    • Press "YES" again to begin the quiz.

Game Mechanics:

  • A timer starts as soon as the quiz begins.
  • The Team must answer The question To Move Towards The Other Questions.
  • You can't skip or go back to previous questions For Re-Attempting.
  • Points are awarded for every correct answer.
  • No negative marking—so take your best shot!

Rules:

  • No calculators, phones, or outside help allowed.
  • You may only discuss with your teammate—no helping other teams.
  • Use only your math skills, logic, and teamwork.
  • Maintain respectful behavior throughout the activity.

Winning Criteria:

The team with the highest score at the end of the quiz wins. If multiple teams have the same score, the fastest team among them (based on time taken to finish) will be declared the winner.
